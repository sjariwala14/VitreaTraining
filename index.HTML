Key Insights from Global Training

Consistent Request for Longer Training

Across all regions and years, 84% of participants requested longer training sessions to better absorb complex technical content. This was the most frequent feedback in Thailand (2023: 76%, 2024: 82%) and Brazil (2023: 94%).

Hands-on Practice is Highly Valued

Hands-on installation exercises were the most praised component across all training sessions. Participants in Thailand (92%) and Brazil (88%) specifically highlighted practical exercises as the most valuable aspect of training.

Cloud Deployment Content Stands Out

AWS deployment content received exceptional feedback, with 87% of participants mentioning it as particularly relevant to current industry trends. This was especially notable in Thailand 2023 where it was unexpectedly well-received.

Experience Level Affects Training Value

Participants with prior Vitrea experience benefited most from advanced troubleshooting sessions, while newcomers requested more fundamental training. This highlights the need for pre-training assessments to tailor content.
